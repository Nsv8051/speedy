## 📖 Overview

**Speedy** is an offline AI desktop assistant designed to make interacting with your computer faster, smarter, and more natural through voice.

Unlike cloud-based assistants, Speedy is built with a **local-first approach**, allowing users to perform everyday tasks while keeping their data on their own machine.

The vision behind Speedy is to create an intelligent desktop companion that combines AI, speech recognition, and desktop automation into a single, seamless experience.

---

## 🎯 Why Speedy?

Many AI assistants rely heavily on cloud services, resulting in internet dependency, increased latency, and privacy concerns.

Speedy aims to provide an alternative by focusing on:

- 🔒 Privacy-first design
- ⚡ Fast local execution
- 🖥️ Desktop automation
- 🎙️ Natural voice interaction
- 🧠 Local AI intelligence
- 🌐 Online capabilities when required
